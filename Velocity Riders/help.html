Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a motorcycle through VelocityRiders? +

To rent a motorcycle, simply use our filter system to select your city, preferred brand, and model. Browse through available options, compare prices, and book directly with verified dealers. You'll need a valid driving license and identity proof for the rental process.

What documents do I need to rent a motorcycle? +

You'll need a valid driving license (with two-wheeler endorsement), government-issued photo ID (Aadhar, PAN, or Passport), and a security deposit. Some dealers may also require proof of address and a credit/debit card for security purposes.

Are the motorcycles insured? +

Yes, all motorcycles listed on VelocityRiders come with comprehensive insurance coverage. However, you may be liable for any damage beyond normal wear and tear. We recommend checking the specific insurance terms with each dealer before booking.

Can I cancel or modify my booking? +

Cancellation and modification policies vary by dealer. Most allow free cancellation up to 24 hours before the rental start time. For modifications, contact the dealer directly through our platform or call our support team for assistance.

What if I face issues during my rental period? +

In case of breakdowns or accidents, immediately contact the dealer and our 24/7 support helpline. Most dealers provide roadside assistance. Never attempt major repairs yourself, as it may void the insurance coverage.

How are rental prices determined? +

Rental prices depend on factors like motorcycle model, rental duration, location, season, and demand. Premium bikes and peak seasons typically cost more. All prices are set by individual dealers and displayed transparently on our platform.
